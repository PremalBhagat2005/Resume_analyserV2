━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. ATS SCORE CALCULATION (0-100)
   • Contact Information: 20 points (name, email, phone)
   • Skills Section: 20 points (dedicated skills detected)
   • Education Section: 15 points (degree/certification)
   • Experience Section: 15 points (work history)
   • Action Verbs & Keywords: 20 points (20+ verbs detected)
   • Resume Length: 10 points (300-800 word optimal)

2. ENTITY EXTRACTION (NER Model)
   • Automatically identifies name, email, phone
   • Extracts skills, education, experience
   • Uses: yashpwr/resume-ner-bert-v2

3. JOB MATCHING (Similarity Model)
   • Calculates semantic similarity (0-100%)
   • Identifies matched keywords
   • Suggests missing keywords
   • Shows strength and improvement areas
   • Uses: anass1209/resume-job-matcher-all-MiniLM-L6-v2

4. IMPROVEMENT SUGGESTIONS
   • Actionable recommendations
   • Missing section alerts
   • Keyword suggestions
   • Best practices tips
